MOAT IoT?

MOAT IoT is a set of APIs/specs to build an IoT/M2M Application on top of ServiceSync Platform.

The consistent and integrated way of the IoT/M2M application development is described in this section.

With the API and Spec:
- You can CREATE your IoT services on the web. - You can ADD new IoT functionality to your existing service.
- You can FOCUS ON your service application, rather than the underlying things.
